Introduction

Welcome to my interaction design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our user.

Gamification is about applying game design ideas and principles to non-game contexts, such as education. With objective being, making it more engaging and fun to use, while motivating user to learn more.

This project involves various phases, to implement this. Such as:

  • Competetive analysis:
    Helps us identify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, highlighting areas we can improve.
  • Wireframing and Prototyping:
    Allows us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring smooth and enjoyable experience.
  • Usability testing:
    Conducted to gather feedback from users on the apps usability and functionality. Used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the user needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimately improving the users learning outcomes.
